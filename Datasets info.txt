BOREAS Dataset: A seasonal collection of self-driving vehicles dataset containing Velodyne Alpha-Prime, a FLIR Blackfly S, commercial Navtech CIR304-H radar, and Applanix POS LV GNSS-INS. It contains 3D cuboids and a 350km distance of traveling information, making the data useful for object perception and recognition.
